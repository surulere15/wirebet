# OPERATION: WIREBET MASTER PLAN

## Mission
To launch `wirebet.com` as a community-owned Web3 betting protocol with zero initial capital, leveraging a powerful narrative and a grassroots community to bootstrap the token and, subsequently, the platform.

---

## Phase 0: Foundation (Complete)

*   **[X] Strategic Framework:** Zero-capital, community-intensive launch model selected.
*   **[X] Brand Reconnaissance:** Digital territory `wirebet` confirmed as clear and available.
*   **[X] Core Narrative:** "Wirebet: The betting platform owned by its players, not a corporation."

---

## Phase 1: Community Genesis (Current Phase: EXECUTE)

*   **Objective:** Build a core community of 100-500 dedicated followers (`True Believers`).
*   **Primary Channels:** Twitter/X, Telegram.
*   **Actions:**
    1.  **Establish Comms:** Create the official `wirebet` Twitter and Telegram accounts.
    2.  **Define Visual Identity:** Create a simple, powerful logo and branding theme.
    3.  **Content Cadence - The Narrative War:**
        *   **Teaser Posts (Week 1):** Post the mission statement. Hint at the problem with traditional betting platforms (high fees, corporate control).
        *   **Vision Posts (Week 2):** Share mockups of the future platform. Run polls asking the community what features they want to see first (e.g., "Sports vs. E-sports?").
        *   **Transparency Posts (Ongoing):** Be radically transparent. "We have $0 in funding. This project will be built by you, for you. The token launch will be 100% community-funded." Frame this as a strength.

---

## Phase 2: The Ignition Event (Awaiting Go-Signal from Phase 1)

*   **Objective:** Launch the `$WIRE` token.
*   **Chosen Chain:** **Base**. Rationale: Low transaction fees, strong narrative (Coinbase backing), and a thriving ecosystem for community-driven projects.
*   **Chosen Launch Model:** **PinkSale Pre-Sale.**
*   **Actions:**
    1.  **Token Contract:** Deploy a standard ERC-20 token contract for `$WIRE` on the Base network.
    2.  **Smart Contract Audit:** Use a low-cost audit service to verify the basic safety of the token contract. This is a critical trust signal.
    3.  **PinkSale Setup:**
        *   Create a pre-sale page for `$WIRE`.
        *   Set a low, achievable fundraise goal (e.g., 3-5 ETH).
        *   **Crucially:** Configure the pre-sale so that 70-100% of the funds raised are automatically paired with `$WIRE` tokens and added to a DEX (like Uniswap v3 on Base) as **locked liquidity** for a minimum of one year.
    4.  **Launch Announcement:** Announce the pre-sale date to the community. Drive all engagement towards this single event.

---

## Phase 3: Protocol Development (Post-Ignition)

*   **Objective:** Build the `wirebet.com` MVP.
*   **Actions:**
    1.  **Secure Treasury:** The small percentage of funds from the pre-sale not allocated to liquidity becomes the initial project treasury.
    2.  **Hire Core Developer:** Use the treasury and a significant allocation of `$WIRE` tokens to hire a skilled smart contract developer.
    3.  **Define Token Utility:** Formalize the use case for `$WIRE` (e.g., fee sharing, governance votes on new betting markets, reduced fees for holders).
    4.  **Build in Public:** Continue the transparent development process, providing regular updates to the community to maintain momentum.
